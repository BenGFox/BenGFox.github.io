Overheat Mechanic Frame Heat Added Adds to Heat by Frame Heat Reduced Reduces Overheat Notes
Elliana's mech will Overheat once it reaches a value of 1200. These are the moves that will effect the heat value. Note that being Overheated disables Elliana's Hover, Dash Attack & FTilt hold variations and every special except USpecial. It also empowers her strong attacks. Elliana's Overheat mechanic freezes while she is in hitstun, preventing her from building or reducing it for this period of time.
Hover 4, 7 (Loops) 12 (Each frame, 84 total per loop) Adds heat on a loop, with 12 heat being accumulated each frame. 48 heat is added on frame 4, 36 heat added on frame 7. You can Hover during Aerials which also adds heat.
Hover (Attack) 4, 7 (Loops) 36 (Each frame, 252 total per loop) Hover Aerials/Specials/Strongs add heat 3x faster than the normal Hover rate.
Dash Attack (Hold) 13, 19 (& first active frame of all following hits) 200 Adds 200 heat per cycle.
FTilt (Hold) 22, 34, 46, 58, 70, 82, 94, 107 200 Adds 200 heat per cycle.
Strongs (Uncharged) (2nd active frame) 150 Overheat will stop reducing during the startup/charge of Elliana's Overheated Strongs. Consecutive Strongs in the air without landing reduces the amount of heat reduced.

Heat Reduction Over Time 13, 25 (Loops) (Approx 2.8 each frame, 70 per loop) Reduces heat on a loop, on frame 13 and frame 25.
Heat Reduction Over Time (Airborne) 13, 25 (Loops) (Approx 0.9 each frame, 23 per loop) Reduces heat on a loop, on frame 13 and frame 25. Being in the air will make the heat decay 3 times slower than it will grounded.
Overheated 13, 25 (Loops) (See Above) Reaching the Overheated state has 1 frame of startup and 14 frames of endlag for Overheat values. The 25 frame loop for heat reduction then begins.
USpecial 4 (Full) Resets heat to 0.
